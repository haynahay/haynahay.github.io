Resume

My name is Hayden Mueller, and I am a middle and high school social studies teacher based in Tokyo, Japan. I grew up in rural Ohio in the US, where I attended high school and graduated concurrently with an Associate of Science Degree from Northwest State Community College in 2015. From there, I received the National College Match Scholarship and attended Trinity College in Hartford, CT from 2015-2019. I graduated from Trinity with a Bachelor of Arts Degree in German Studies and Theater & Dance.
I am currently enrolled in my Master’s in Education from the University of the People, which I expect to complete in October 2021. I am also a prospective applicant to the University of Missouri’s Master of Science in Learning Technologies and Design with a focus in Technology in Schools.
